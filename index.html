What is Krossover Kompete?

An opportunity for athletes

Krossover Kompete is a competition for high school atheltes with game film and data on the Krossover platform. They will enter for a chance to be selected to participate at a week-long basketball clinic in New York City and play an exhibition game in front of fans, family, coaches, and college recruiters at Madison Square Garden, the world's most famous arena.

And an opportunity for you

We know that as a college recruiter you're busy. You don't have the budget to travel up and down the nation to scout out diamonds in the rough that may or may not pan out. Krossover Select tracks and breaks down the games of all your recruits so you could have an intelligence edge. Throughout the Krossover Kompete selection process, you can keep an eye on the most promising underdogs out there. At the Krossover Klinic, you will witness these players in one place, at the top of their game .

How it works

Athletes Apply

Athletes or coaches will provide us with footage of the player's game. After submission, the Krossover platform will extract insights and analyze each player's accumulated data for selection into Krossover Kompete.

Krossover footage

Krossover footage breaks each event from the game with its own video clip tagged with play information and stats from the footage. We provide intelligence to break down the game in a way that lets coaches and players learn the game.

Selection Process

There are three draft rounds. The first selects 500 top players at the local level. The second selects 200 to go on to the regional selection. In the third round, a panel of select coaches selects the top 18 to enter the Klinic.

Krossover Klinic

At this week-long event, rising ballers from across the country meet and learn the art of the game from the top coaches and players nationwide, culminating in a showcase game seen by fans and college recruiters live and via telecast.
